About us

Never worry about finding a clean, accessible restroom again! FlushFinder is your go-to app for locating nearby public washrooms wherever you are. Whether you're in a bustling city or exploring a new area, this user-friendly app helps you find the nearest and most suitable facilities in seconds.

Why FlushFinder?

FlushFinder isn't just about finding the nearest washroom; it's about enhancing your comfort and peace of mind during your day-to-day activities. Whether you're a traveler, a parent with young kids, someone with specific accessibility needs, or simply seeking a clean and convenient restroom, FlushFinder has got you covered.
